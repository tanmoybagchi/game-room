🔺 Pyramid

🏠

How to Play

Goal: Remove all cards from the pyramid by pairing cards that add up to 13.

Pairing
Select two exposed cards whose ranks total 13. Kings (13) are removed alone. A = 1, J = 11, Q = 12, K = 13.
Exposed Cards
A card is exposed when no cards overlap it from the row below.
Stock & Waste
Click the stock to draw a card to the waste. The waste card can be paired with any exposed pyramid card.

Controls: Tap a card to select it, then tap another to pair. Tap a King to remove it instantly.

🎉 You Win!
