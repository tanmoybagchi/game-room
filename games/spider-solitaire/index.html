Spider Solitaire

🏠

How to Play

Goal: Build eight complete sequences of King down to Ace in the same suit. Completed sequences are removed automatically.

Tableau (10 columns)
Stack cards in descending order. You can move any descending same-suit sequence. Any card can be placed on an empty column.
Stock (bottom-right)
Click to deal one card to each of the 10 columns. All columns must have at least one card before dealing.
Suits
This version uses a single suit (Spades) for a relaxed game. Build runs of K→A to clear them.

Controls: Drag cards or tap to select then tap a destination. Use Undo to take back a move.

🎉 You Win!
