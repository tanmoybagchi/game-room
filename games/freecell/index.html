How to Play

Goal: Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.

Free Cells (top-left)
Four temporary slots that can each hold one card. Use them strategically to free up moves.
Foundations (top-right)
Build each suit up from Ace to King. Double-click a card to auto-send it.
Tableau (8 columns)
Stack cards in descending order, alternating colors. Any card can be placed on an empty column.
Moving stacks
You can move a sequence of cards if there are enough empty free cells and empty columns to support the move.

Controls: Drag cards or tap to select then tap a destination. Use Undo to take back a move.
