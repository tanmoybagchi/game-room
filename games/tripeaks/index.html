🗻 TriPeaks

🏠

How to Play

Goal: Clear all three peaks by removing cards that are one rank higher or lower than the waste card.

Tableau
Three overlapping peaks of cards. A card is playable when no cards overlap it from the row below.
Playing Cards
Click an exposed card that is ±1 rank from the top waste card. Aces wrap with Kings (A↔K).
Stock
Click the stock pile to draw a new waste card when no moves are available.
Streaks
Chain together multiple cards without drawing from stock for longer combos!

Controls: Tap an exposed card to play it. Tap the stock to draw.

🎉 You Win!
