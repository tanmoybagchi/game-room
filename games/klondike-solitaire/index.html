🃏 Klondike Solitaire

🏠

How to Play

Goal: Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.

Tableau (7 columns)
Stack cards in descending order, alternating colors (e.g. black 8 on red 9). Only Kings can be placed on empty columns.
Stock & Waste (top-left)
Click the stock pile to draw a card. When the stock is empty, click to recycle the waste.
Foundations (top-right)
Build each suit up from Ace to King. Double-click a card to auto-send it.

Controls: Drag cards or tap to select then tap a destination. Use Undo to take back a move.

🎉 You Win!
