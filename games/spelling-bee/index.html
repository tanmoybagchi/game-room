🐝 Spelling Bee

🏠

How to Play

Goal: Make as many words as possible using the 7 letters shown.

Rules
Words must be at least 4 letters long and must include the center (yellow) letter. Letters can be reused.
Scoring
4-letter words = 1 point. Longer words = 1 point per letter. Pangrams (using all 7 letters) earn 7 bonus points!
Ranks
Beginner → Good → Nice → Great → Amazing → Genius → Queen Bee (100%).
