How to Play

Goal: Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King.

Tableau (7 columns)
Stack cards in descending order, alternating colors. Unlike Klondike, any face-up card can be moved — the cards below it come along, even if not in sequence.
Empty Columns
Only Kings (with any cards on top) can be placed on empty columns.
Foundations
Build each suit up from Ace to King. Double-click a card to auto-send it.
No Stock
All cards are dealt at the start — there is no stock pile. Plan your moves carefully!

Controls: Drag cards or tap to select then tap a destination. Use Undo to take back a move.
