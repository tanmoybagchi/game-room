💣 Minesweeper

🏠

How to Play

Goal: Reveal all cells without hitting a mine.

Reveal
Tap a cell to reveal it. Numbers show how many mines are adjacent.
Flag
Toggle 🚩 mode then tap to mark suspected mines. Or long-press a cell.
Cascade
Revealing a 0 automatically reveals all adjacent safe cells.
