How to Play

Goal: Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries.

🟩 Green
The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
🟨 Yellow
The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
⬛ Gray
The letter is not in the word at all.
Typing
Use the on-screen keyboard or your physical keyboard. Press Enter to submit, Backspace to delete.
