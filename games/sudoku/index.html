🔢 Sudoku

🏠

How to Play

Goal: Fill every cell so each row, column, and 3×3 box contains the digits 1–9 exactly once.

Placing Numbers
Tap a cell, then tap a number from the pad below. Tap the same number again to clear it.
Notes Mode
Toggle ✏️ to enter pencil marks — small candidate numbers in a cell.
Errors
Conflicting numbers are highlighted in red. Fix them to continue.
Difficulty
Choose Easy, Medium, Hard, or Expert. Harder levels reveal fewer starting numbers.
